# Phase 08 - HR and Behavioral Questions

## A) Motivation and Career Fit

1. Why are you looking for a job right now?
2. Why this company and this role specifically?
3. Where do you see yourself in 2-3 years?
4. What kind of team environment helps you do your best work?
5. What motivates you more: ownership, learning, impact, or compensation?
6. Why should we choose you as a fresher?
7. What does success in your first 6 months look like?
8. Are you open to relocation and hybrid/on-site requirements?

## B) Strengths, Weaknesses, and Learning

9. What are your top strengths with evidence from projects?
10. What is one weakness and what are you doing to improve it?
11. Tell me about a time you learned a new tool quickly.
12. How do you react when you are stuck technically?
13. How do you handle feedback from seniors/reviewers?
14. How do you prioritize when multiple deadlines collide?
15. What is your approach to continuous learning after joining?

## C) Teamwork and Communication

16. Tell me about a conflict in a project and how you handled it.
17. How do you communicate technical tradeoffs to non-technical people?
18. Have you ever disagreed with a teammate's implementation approach?
19. How do you ensure smooth handover of your work?
20. How do you document decisions so others can continue easily?
21. What do you do when requirements change late in development?
22. How do you ask for help without blocking progress?

## D) Ownership and Problem Solving Stories

23. Tell me about a bug that took longer than expected to fix.
24. Tell me about a production-like failure and your response.
25. Tell me about a decision you made with incomplete information.
26. Tell me about a time you improved performance or reliability.
27. Tell me about a feature you are proud of and why.
28. Tell me about a time you had to cut scope.
29. Tell me about a time you made a wrong assumption.
30. Tell me about how you validated a major project claim/metric.

## E) Fresher-Specific Situations

31. You have no full-time experience; why should we trust your execution?
32. How will you adapt from student projects to production engineering standards?
33. How do you handle pressure during placement season and job uncertainty?
34. How do you balance speed and quality as a junior engineer?
35. What kind of mentorship do you expect from your first manager?
36. How do you plan to contribute in the first month if onboarding is fast?
37. If assigned legacy code with no docs, how will you proceed?
38. If your first task is outside your comfort zone, what is your plan?

## F) Closing Questions

39. Do you have any questions for us?
40. What compensation expectations do you have and why?
41. If selected, when can you join?
42. Is there anything else you want us to know about your profile?
